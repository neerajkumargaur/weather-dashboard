About Aero

Aero is a next-generation weather dashboard built to deliver real-time atmospheric data with a visually stunning interface.

🌍 Our Mission

Our mission is to transform how people interact with weather data. Traditional weather apps are cluttered and outdated — Aero focuses on clarity, design, and accuracy.

⚡ What We Offer

  • Real-Time Weather

    Live weather updates powered by advanced forecasting models and global datasets.

  • Premium User Experience

    Smooth animations, glassmorphism UI, and modern layouts designed for usability.

  • Weather Blogs

    Explore in-depth articles about climate change, weather science, and environmental insights.

  • Accurate Forecasting

    Data sourced from Open-Meteo ensuring high accuracy and reliability.

🔬 Technology Stack

Aero is built using modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and API-driven architecture. It is designed to be scalable and easily extendable into full-stack applications.

🚀 Future Scope

We plan to integrate AI-based weather predictions, location-based services, and personalized weather alerts to enhance user experience further.

📌 Why Aero?

Aero combines design and data to deliver a seamless experience. It’s not just a weather app — it’s a platform for understanding the environment around you.
